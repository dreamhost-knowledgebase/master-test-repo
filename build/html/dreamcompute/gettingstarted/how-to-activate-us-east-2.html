How To Activate DreamCompute zone US-East 2

Note

These instructions are valid for customers of DreamCompute Beta. If you have joined DreamCompute after March 30 2016 these instructions are not valid for you.

Once you’re ready to switch to the new billing system and make full use of the faster DreamCompute zone, login into DreamHost Control Panel and click on the button Activate now to start the wizard.

Note

Adding US-East 2 as a new cluster to your DreamCompute plan requires also changing your billing system: please make sure to read how your bill will change before proceeding.

Confirm activation and you’re done. You can use the new password to login into the US-East 2 dashboard.
